Q: What documents are needed for exporting goods?
A: Commercial invoice, packing list, bill of lading, certificate of origin, and export license.

Q: What is an export license?
A: An export license is a government document that authorizes the export of specific goods in specific quantities to a particular destination.

Q: What is the Harmonized System code?
A: The HS code is an internationally standardized system of names and numbers to classify traded products.
